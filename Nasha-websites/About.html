Nasha Resin Crafts

Introduction

At Nasha Resin Crafts, I create functional art by capturing fleeting natural beauty in durable, crystal-clear resin

The Story behind & The Maker

Hi, I'm Nagaba Shallot, "Nasha". My fascination with resin started with a simple goal: To capture the depth of the ocean and to freeze a single flower in time. What began as a hobby in my space room quickly turned into a passion for creating pieces that are as durable as they are beautiful. At Nasha Resin Crafts, I believe that "functional" should never mean "boring."

The Process

Working with resin is a delicate dance of chemistry and creativity. Each piece undergoes a multi-day journey:

The Pour:we hand-mix professional-grade, UV and A:B resin with pigments.

The Cure:A patient 24 to 72-hour curing process ensures a rock-solid, glass-like finish.

The Detail:Every edge is hand-sanded and polished until it glows. Whether it's a custom floral preservation block or a shimmering ocean tray, I treat every pour as if it were staying in my own home.

Why Choose Nasha Resin Crafts?

In a world of mass-produced decor, we focus on the "one-of-one." Because resin is a fluid medium, no two cells or swirls are ever exactly the same. When you buy from me, you aren't just getting a product; you're getting a handcrafted slice of coastal serenity and botanical elegance that is built to last a lifetime.

The Mission

Our mission is to transform your most fleeting, meaningful moments into timeless heirlooms. By blending meticulous preservation techniques with the clarity of artisan resin, we ensure that the beauty of your story never fades.
